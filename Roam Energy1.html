Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about solar energy solutions

The cost of a solar system depends on several factors including your energy needs, the size of the system, and whether you need battery storage. Residential systems typically start from $2,000 for a basic setup, while commercial systems can range from $10,000 to $100,000+. Contact us for a free customized quote based on your specific requirements.

We offer comprehensive warranties on all our products and installations:

  • Solar panels: 25-30 years performance warranty
  • Batteries: 10 years warranty
  • Inverters: 5 years warranty
  • Installation workmanship: 1 year warranty

Installation time varies depending on the system size and complexity:

  • Residential systems: 1-3 days
  • Commercial systems: 1-6 weeks
  • Large-scale projects: 4-12 weeks

After your initial consultation and system design approval, we'll provide a detailed timeline for your specific project.

Solar systems require minimal maintenance. We recommend:

  • Visual inspection every 6 months
  • Cleaning panels 2-4 times per year (more in dusty areas)
  • Professional system check every 2-3 years

We offer affordable maintenance packages to keep your system performing at its best.

  • Energy-efficient appliances to minimize consumption
